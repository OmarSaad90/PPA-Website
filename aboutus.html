Meet Our Founder

Charbel Abou Samra

Founder & Principal Consultant

Charbel advises clients across the full spectrum of project and dispute challenges. With more than 15 years of industry experience, he has built a career that bridges field construction, project management, and consulting.

His expertise spans constructability reviews, project controls, commercial advisory, and construction claims and dispute resolution, with a focus on forensic schedule and quantum analysis. His project portfolio includes infrastructure, energy, power and water, mining, and marine works.

Our Story

PPA was founded to provide independent, evidence-based solutions in construction claims, project controls, and dispute resolution. Our team brings a unique blend of field experience, project management expertise, and consulting knowledge, enabling us to deliver advice that is both practical and defensible.

Having managed projects on the ground, directed commercial and scheduling teams, and advised on high-stakes disputes, we understand challenges from every perspective.
