Learn More About Our Services →

Constructability Review

We conduct rigorous constructability reviews to identify design gaps, sequencing challenges, and potential risks before construction begins. Our independent assessments reduce risk exposure and provide clarity for Owners and Contractors.

Project Controls

We deliver integrated project controls solutions that combine cost management, scheduling, and risk analysis. With accurate forecasting and real-time reporting, we provide clients with the insights they need to make informed decisions.

Commercial Advisory

We advise clients on contract strategy, risk allocation, and commercial management throughout the project lifecycle. By blending technical and financial expertise, we help protect margins and strengthen clients' commercial positions.

Construction Claims & Dispute Resolution

We prepare, analyze, and defend claims related to delay, disruption, productivity loss, and additional costs. Our experts specialize in forensic schedule analysis and quantum evaluation, producing clear, evidence-based reports.

Surety and Insurance Support

We support surety companies, insurers, and their counsel by conducting independent project audits, performance bond evaluations, and claim investigations. Our assessments provide objective clarity on liability, quantum, and delays.

Professional Training & Development

We deliver tailored training programs on project controls, claims management, and dispute resolution. Our courses combine real-world case studies with industry best practices, equipping professionals with essential skills.
