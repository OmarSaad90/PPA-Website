Our Services

Comprehensive solutions designed to ensure your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and with minimal risk.

Constructability Review

We perform independent constructability reviews to detect design gaps, sequencing challenges, and hidden risks before work begins. Our assessments help Owners and Contractors reduce exposure, avoid costly rework, and deliver projects with greater certainty.

Project Controls

We provide integrated cost, schedule, and risk management solutions. With accurate forecasting and real-time reporting, we give clients the visibility and control they need to stay on budget, anticipate challenges, and make informed decisions.

Commercial Advisory

We guide clients on contract strategy, risk allocation, and commercial management throughout the project lifecycle. By combining technical and financial insight, we help safeguard margins, strengthen negotiating positions, and support successful project outcomes.

Construction Claims & Dispute Resolution

We analyze, prepare, and defend claims for delay, disruption, productivity loss, and additional costs. Our specialists in forensic schedule and quantum analysis deliver evidence-based reports that support fair resolution and stand up to scrutiny.

Surety and Insurance Support

We assist sureties, insurers, and counsel with independent audits, and claim investigations. Our objective assessments bring clarity on liability, cost, and delay, enabling faster decisions and defensible outcomes.

Professional Training & Development

We design and deliver training programs in project controls, claims, and dispute resolution. Using real-world case studies and best practices, we equip professionals with practical skills that improve project delivery and reduce disputes.
